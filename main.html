Project Wildspace

About us

We are a team of 3 computer science students from the University of Central Florida who are excited to be competing in Knight Hacks 2023.

Problem statement

Dungeon Masters in D&D struggle with impromptu content creation like NPCs, locations, and quests during gameplay. This constant need for real-time improvisation reduces DMs' ability to focus on facilitating the actual adventure. The pressure of on-the-fly thinking also leads to inconsistent worldbuilding and lack of narrative cohesion in campaigns. Our project aims to mitigate these issues that Dungeon Masters face.

What is Wildspace

Wildspace is a graphical user interface (GUI) application built using Python that utilizes the power of large language models to dynamically generate roleplaying game content for Dungeon Masters (DMs) in Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) gameplay sessions.
