Name: The Arcane Resistance
Number of Members: 5,000
Devotion: Medium
Morality: Chaotic Good
Goal: The Arcane Resistance seeks to free magic users from the oppressive rule of Morellus and establish a society that embraces and celebrates the use of magic.
Features: Members of The Arcane Resistance wear vibrant purple cloaks adorned with silver stars, and their symbol is a stylized wand surrounded by flames. They often greet each other with the phrase 'May your magic burn bright.'
Activities: The Arcane Resistance operates in secret, organizing underground meetings and protests to raise awareness about the plight of magic users. They also provide support and protection to those who have been persecuted by the Morellus government.
